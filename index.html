How to throw 67

Show two hands at once: one hand as “6”, the other as “7”.

6 (shaka / hang loose): Thumb and pinky extended, index / middle / ring fingers curled.

7: Thumb, index, and pinky extended; middle and ring fingers curled.

When both are detected together, you’ll see a green flash and the counter will go up (with a short cooldown between counts).
